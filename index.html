PocketLog

Privacy Policy

PocketLog · Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

PocketLog is a personal expense tracking app built for simplicity and privacy. Your financial data stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

Data Collection

PocketLog does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account creation or sign-up is required.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is collected.
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers are included.
  • No data is sent over the internet. The app works entirely offline.

Data Storage

All transaction data, preferences (currency, language, theme), and settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (MMKV / AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it using the in-app export feature.

Data Export & Import

PocketLog allows you to export your transactions as a JSON file and import data from a JSON file. These operations are initiated entirely by you and use your device's native share sheet. No data is uploaded to any server during this process.

Permissions

PocketLog requests only the minimum permissions necessary:

  • Internet – Required by React Native for development builds. The production app does not make any network requests.
  • Storage (Android 9 and below) – Used for exporting data to files on older Android versions.

Third-Party Services

PocketLog does not integrate with any third-party services, cloud providers, or external APIs. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, and no server-side components.

Children's Privacy

PocketLog does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not collect information from any user regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since PocketLog does not collect any data, changes are unlikely but we will keep this policy current.
